Our Story

Meet Hannah

Established in 2018 by Hannah D., Be-Ti-Ful Salon & Spa is the culmination of years of passion for beauty. Hannah opened her first salon, "Top To Toes Beauty Salon," in Hayward in 2005, followed by a second location in San Jose in 2012. Now on Murphy Avenue in Sunnyvale, Hannah and her team are dedicated to making every client look and feel their absolute best.
